About Me

My passion for Web Development started during college where I worked on multiple websites for my courses and a WordPress website for a non-for-profit organization, UNNA. I was also a teaching assistant for an advanced programming course during my time at UAlbany. I graduated from the University at Albany in May 2018 with a B.S. in Informatics, a Minor in Business, and a 3.8 GPA, receiving the highest honors available.

As my first job after college, I was an Information Technology Specialist where my job duties included managing their Jotform forms and multiple websites, mostly through WordPress. I then became a Business and Systems Analyst at Canon USA for Application Operations and Deployment. During my time there, I used tools such as Atlassian's JIRA and Confluence, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and frequently tested for Quality Assurance while being in close liason with both the business/customers and the developers.
