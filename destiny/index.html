Destiny Mini-Game: By Kevin Mitchell

Click Image to Start

Objective:

Guardian, Oryx is wreaking havoc all over the Destiny universe. Track him down and destroy anybody and anything in your way!

How to Play:

  1. First, you will make your character. Each class has different abilities called "Supers".

  2. During battles, you will use the Attack button to attack enemies. You can use your Super once per battle to utilize your unique Guardian Ability.

  3. After each battle, follow the instructions presented to you or browse through the shop!

  4. Oh, lastly, survive!
