About
For one of my senior projects in college, I created a 5-page website dedicated to Kobe Bryant as he was a big inspiration to my life. After I graduated, the files were deleted off the server and the hard drive with the files became corrupted. Kobe's passing inspired me to create another from scratch showcasing Kobe's Mamba Mentality, his motivation, and his greatness along with some of my web development skills.
This generates a random quote from Kobe and a random background about him. I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Thank you Kobe!
