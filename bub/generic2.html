Babygirl, Boobie, Bub

May 2020 by Kevin Mitchell

Happy May babe! I hope you will enjoy and cherish that You Matter hoodie that I got for our anniversary! You Matter are two very important words put together that we should all say to ourselves whether we're up or down. Everytime I see your gracious smile, it reminds me that I matter, too; that I can help put a smile on someone else's face. That has always been important to me and to see that on you doubles that feeling and should cause you to think the same way. The same way you post the pick-me-up pictures on Instagram, reminding people that they matter, this is a personal one for you.

Enjoy family, friends, life, and us, we all love you (especially me)! Every weekend has been filled with love, joy, care, passion, and adventure even though we're in this square 💚! God bless always!
