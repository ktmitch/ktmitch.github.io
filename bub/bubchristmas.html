Bub and Doodle

December 25, 2020 by Kevin Mitchell

Merry Christmas Bub Bub! Christmas reminds one of the people close enough to the person. Those close usually get some sort of gift whether monetary or not, tangible or intangible in some way. You and I show our appreciation for each other on this day like we do on many days, today is just magnified. This is a celebration, not only for Christmas itself, but for us celebrating another one. Merry Christmas my love!

  • "Christmas will always be as long as we stand heart to heart and hand in hand."
  • "And that, of course, is the message of Christmas. We are never alone. Not when the night is darkest, the wind coldest, the world seemingly most indifferent..."
  • “Christmas is forever, not for just one day. For loving, sharing, giving, are not to put away.”

Enjoy the Christmas day! Can't wait to see you 💚! God bless you and your family always!
