Babygirl, Boobie, Bub

April 2020 by Kevin Mitchell

My baby is truly one of a kind. Intelligent, funny as hell, and as caring as a mom to her newborn child. These are only 3 reasons why I wanted to keep her in my daily life since we started talking.

There's not many girls that would do what she has done for me and hold me down with her spunk. I need to remind myself of what she has done, even though our situations are different in some aspects.

One thing about her is that she's a go-getter. From when we started talking to now, she's graduated college with a great GPA, she's gotten called for interviews for various tough positions, interned and eventually started working two jobs, and obtained her driving license. She started drawing again, as I look at my professional-like drawing of Pain (watch Naruto!), and started her Youtube channel after hesitating for years. I think I will use this time to tell her that she motivates me daily to do my things and things I've thought of and I'm very thankful for each joule of energy she has put into that.
